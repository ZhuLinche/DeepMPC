chebyCenter

Purpose

Compute the Chebyshev centre of the Polyhedron.

Syntax

s = P.chebyCenter
s = P.chebyCenter(facet)
s = P.chebyCenter(facet,bound)
s = chebyCenter(P,facet,bound)

Description

Computes the Chebyshev centre of a polyhedron via solving the following optimization problem

where is the centre of the ball inscribed inside the H-representation of the polyhedron P and is the radius of the ball. The polyhedron is given as with inequalities and equalities. It is possible to compute the Chebyshev centre restricted to one or more facets of polyhedron by providing vector of indices facet. Note that the polyhedron must be in its minimal representation in order to have facets. If the facet indices are provided, the inequalities in the above optimization problem belonging to these indices change to equality constraints. It is also possible to upper bound the radius r by specifying bound input. In this case the constraint is added.

Input Arguments

P

Polyhedron in H-representation.

Class: Polyhedron
facet

Vector of indices specifying a subset of facets for computing the Chebyshev centre.

Class: double

Default: []
bound

Upper bound on the radius of the inscribed ball.

Class: double

Default: Inf

Output Arguments

s

Structure with outputs from the Chebyshev centre optimization problem.

Class: struct

s.exitflag

Informs if the optimization problem was terminated properly.

Class: double
s.x

Centre of the ball inscribed in polyhedron P.

Class: double
s.r

Radius of the ball inscribed in polyhedron P.

Class: double
